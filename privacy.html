DATA WE DON'T COLLECT

Your camera feed never leaves your device. All face detection is processed in real time locally using on-device AI. No video is recorded, stored, or transmitted — ever.
  • 🚫
    Camera / VideoProcessed in real time on-device only. Never recorded or uploaded.
  • 🚫
    Biometric DataEye measurements and facial landmarks are computed and immediately discarded per frame.
  • 🚫
    LocationWe never access your GPS location.
  • 🚫
    Health DataAlertness scores are stored locally on your device only — not on our servers.
  • 🚫
    Contacts / MessagesWe never access your contacts, messages, or any other personal data.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • ✓
    Account ManagementCreating and maintaining your account.
  • ✓
    AuthenticationVerifying your identity when you sign in.
  • ✓
    Account DeletionProcessing your request to delete your account and all associated data.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or sale to third parties.

05

STORAGE & SECURITY

Your account data (name and email) is stored securely by Google Firebase, operating data centres compliant with ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and other international security standards. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

All detection data — alertness scores, session logs, and streak history — is stored only on your device using browser localStorage and IndexedDB. This data never reaches our servers.
